(must be paraphrased!!)EEG data was recorded on BrainVision Recorder in a quiet, sound- and electrically-shielded Faraday cage to minimize external noise. We used a 32-channel ActiCAP Ag/AgCl active electrode system and BrainAmp amplifier (Brain Products GmbH, Germany), using the Oz electrode as a drop-down ECG electrode placed on the left lower back, leaving 31-channels for scalp EEG recording. We used a sampling rate of 500 Hz from the electrodes arranged according to the International 10–20 electrode system (see Figure XX), with the FCz electrode as reference and AFz as ground. During the measurement, the electrode impedances were maintained below 25 kΩ. We instructed the participants to relax, minimize movement, and stay awake during all recordings.
